⧉ Neon Vault: 3‑Player Escape

Work together as Decoder, Navigator, and Observer to unlock the vault.
3‑Player Co‑op • Local • Browser‑only

Mission Briefing
Difficulty: Medium
The three of you have broken into a digital vault hidden beneath a neon city. The AI guarding it has split the escape code into three layers: a word key, a number code, and a final phrase. Each player sees different clues. Only by talking and sharing can you escape.

Step 1 — The Word Key

Decoder leads this step. Others: read your role cards and give hints.
On the vault door, three glowing words appear: DESERT · OCEAN · FOREST. The AI says: “Choose the biome where life hides in the fewest places, but survives with the strongest will.”
Navigator and Observer each have extra hints about which biome fits best.
Waiting for the Decoder’s answer…

Step 2 — The Number Code

Navigator leads this step. Others: share your hidden number clues.
The AI reveals a sequence: 3 · 6 · 9 · ? and says: “The next number is not just the next in the pattern. It is the sum of the digits of the year when humans first walked on the Moon.”
Decoder and Observer each know part of the Moon clue. Combine what you know.
Navigator, it’s your move.

Step 3 — The Final Phrase

Observer leads this step. Everyone must share their final clues.
The vault asks for a phrase of three words: “Say what you did to escape: a verb, then the place you chose, then what you unlocked.”
Each role card contains one of the three words (verb, place, or object). Put them together in the right order.
Observer, assemble the final phrase.

Vault Unlocked!

You escaped the Neon Vault together. Nice teamwork.

Tip: Try swapping roles next time and see if you’re faster.
